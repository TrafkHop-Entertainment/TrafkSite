Description

TrafkHop Entertainment™ is an independent one-person studio based in Austria, founded by HoppiTex on December 16th, 2025.
We specialize in gameplay-driven RPGs inspired by classics like Mario & Luigi and Chrono Trigger. Our mission is to create visually stunning experiences that blend a playful early 2000s cartoon/anime aesthetic with deep, meaningful lore, japanese/classical/indie inspired music and austrian culture/volklore into various dirrerent games, that are both fun and interactive. Every title we develop is part of a single, meticulously crafted universe. From our unique, logic-based magic system to the smallest historical detail, we ensure absolute consistency across all of our games. While our heart belongs to RPGs, we aim to create games in every genre in the long run.
Join our journey: Stay updated via Discord, YouTube, and itch.io. Website: https://trafkhop-entertainment.github.io/TrafkSite/

History

TrafkHop Entertainment was founded on the 16th of december 2025 by HoppiTex. He wanted to have a place for his works. Since he began writing his storys in early August(when he drove home from his italy vacation), he wanted a place where he could keep his storys and games. And so just before Chrismas he created TrafkHop. Shortly after he began making the website of his studio, where he wanted a wiki to display and keep all of his storys(and infos). That was a long task, because the website would only go into its final stages in late april of 2026 (still ongoing). After the website if completed, he will focus more on the storys and games.

TrafkHop ist made of Trafk, wich is the equivelant to antimatter for Mana, and Hop, from grasshopper wich means fast, jumpy, agile, fast ig.
