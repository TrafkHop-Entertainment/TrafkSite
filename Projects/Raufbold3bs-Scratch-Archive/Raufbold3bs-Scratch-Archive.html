RSA - Raufbold3b's Scratch Archive is a collection of 15 very old and bad games released by HoppiTex (back then Raufbold3b/Ullidani) on Scratch/Unreleased.

Raufbold3b's Scratch Archive

History

At the time (~2018 - 2024) he did not know how to code and he was also a bit too young to really learn it properly. So he made the best out of Scratch and other modifications like Turbowarp, Penguinmod and Dinosaurmod. Most games were really bad, a few are decent but are really unfinished and bugged. In the glorious year 2025, HoppiTex made the decision to make a collection of his "best" works (that even he dislikes by now) and released it shortly after on Itch.io on 19.12.2025!
